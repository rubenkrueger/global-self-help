MISSION STATEMENT

Connect third world entrepreneurs with experts, resources, and capital in the first world.

THE VISION

Eliminate dependency / Foster the entrepreneurial spirit / Create global teamwork

WHAT WE DO

In developing countries, foreign aid can reinforce the poverty cycle by competing with local economies and rendering homegrown efforts ineffective, Global Self Help looks to support innovators by connecting them with advisers and resources. Whether it is through expert advice or financial patronage -- the power is with the entrepreneur.

GET INVOLVED

1. Donate or Volunteer at GSH
2. Become an adviser for a social entrepreneur
3. Seek help as a social entrepreneur
